# Growth OS

Growth OS is an AI-powered website growth intelligence platform built with n8n.

Instead of generating a simple website audit, Growth OS analyzes a website from multiple business perspectives and produces actionable recommendations, executive summaries, prioritised roadmaps, and AI-generated reports.

---

# Features

## Website Crawling

- HTML extraction
- Metadata collection
- DOM analysis
- Technology detection

---

## Audit Engines

The platform evaluates websites across multiple categories.

- SEO
- UX
- Conversion
- Performance
- Accessibility
- Branding
- Trust
- Security
- Technical Stack
- Content

Each engine produces its own score and opportunities.

---

## Recommendation Engine

Maps detected issues to:

- Priority
- Impact
- Estimated effort
- Time required
- Expected score gain
- Business explanation

---

## Normalizer Engine

Converts all audit outputs into one unified schema.

---

## Strength Ranking Engine

Ranks website strengths from strongest to weakest.

---

## Opportunity Ranking Engine

Ranks issues by:

- Business impact
- Priority
- Expected gain
- Difficulty

---

## Priority Engine

Creates:

- Priority fixes
- Quick wins
- High impact recommendations

---

## Business Impact Engine

Explains why every issue matters from a business perspective.

---

## Benchmark Engine

Compares website performance against industry averages.

Example:

- Startup
- SaaS
- Agency
- Ecommerce
- Healthcare

---

## Opportunity Engine

Calculates:

- Total issues
- Estimated score increase
- Opportunity size

---

## Risk Engine

Identifies business risks including:

- SEO risk
- Conversion risk
- Security risk
- Brand risk

---

## Maturity Engine

Determines business maturity.

Example:

- Beginner
- Growing
- Scaling
- Market Leader

---

## Code Insight Engine

Provides technical implementation advice for developers.

---

## Roadmap Engine

Automatically groups improvements into:

### Immediate

High impact fixes.

### Short Term

Growth improvements.

### Long Term

Strategic optimisation.

---

## Executive Dashboard

Produces executive KPIs including:

- Overall score
- Grade
- Health status
- Category scores
- Priority fixes
- Roadmap
- Benchmark
- Maturity

---

## AI Report

The final stage sends all structured data into an AI model which generates a client-ready audit report.
